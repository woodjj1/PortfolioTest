Me and my story


As a current student studying a Bachelor's degree in I.T. at Otago Polytechnic, I have developed a passion for learning everything that is computers. After working in the Engineering industry for 11 years, I decided to pursue a career in the I.T. sector.

I try my best in all aspects of life and believe I feel in my element when dealing with people of all ages, cultures and walks of life. I was awarded Otago Polytechnic's most promising first year in 2016 which was a great honor.

PERSONAL INTERESTS
When I'm not studying, you'll find me with my family enjoying being a dad to two beautiful girls aged 8 and 6.

I enjoy music and djing in particular and have been for over 15 years. I dj all types of events from Private functions, weddings, nightclubs and I was the opening dj act for the band Six60 in 2017..
